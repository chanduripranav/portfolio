Electron React FastAPI Groq Llama 3.3 FAISS Neo4j WebSocket

Desktop meeting assistant that prepares you before meetings, surfaces action items and decisions during them, and drafts follow-up emails after — governed by PRISM, a self-improving intervention gate that decides when to speak up and, just as importantly, when to stay quiet.

  • Human-in-the-loop follow-up emails — drafted, never auto-sent
